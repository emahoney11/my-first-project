# Fun Facts 🎉

## Random & Interesting Tidbits

1. **Tech Fact**: The first computer bug was an actual moth found in a Harvard Mark II computer in 1947!

2. **Space Fact**: A day on Venus is longer than its year. Venus takes 243 Earth days to rotate once but only 225 Earth days to orbit the Sun.

3. **Animal Fact**: Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.

4. **Food Fact**: Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found 3,000-year-old honey in Egyptian tombs that's still perfectly edible.

5. **Ocean Fact**: We've explored less than 5% of Earth's oceans, meaning we know more about the surface of Mars than our own ocean floor.

6. **History Fact**: The shortest war in history was between Britain and Zanzibar in 1896. It lasted only 38 minutes!
